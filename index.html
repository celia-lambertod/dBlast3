About us

We are a group of 7 students from IESEG School of management in La Défense, Paris.

As we are all passionate about electronic music, we want our school community to discover new musical styles from EDM to Hardcore. We want to break stereotypes about these music genres and provide new kinds of events for all tastes.

Parity

Another aim of our association is to help women have more responsibility in projects. More generally we want to show our student community that the techno music universe is ahead in term of parity.

Activities

Techno Afterwork

As a brand new association, we will bring new event formats to students. Our first event of 2020 will be in a pub in Paris, bringing local DJs to the party.

Offers and giveaways

Contests will be held for students to win tickets for music events on our student Facebook group. We will also give discounts for events. More than 5000 students are part of this Facebook community.

Prevention

We want all students to feel safe each time they get to any of our events. To do so, we teamed up with IESEG's association Party Warning to help student learn more about drugs, alcohol and STIs.

Partners

We strive to get the best partners for students to have the best offers for memorable events.
